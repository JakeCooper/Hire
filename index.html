What is Hire?

Hire is an community driven job search platform. Through the application, users can hire others in their area to do random jobs for them. Some of the features available to users:

  • Chatting between a user and a worker.
  • A rating system that allows a user to rate a worker with comments.
  • A profile page, and the ability to show whether you are searching for work, or seeking help.
  • An areas to highlight skills, if you are searching for work.
  • A job description page for each job.
  • A safe payment system.
